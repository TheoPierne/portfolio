Finger dissociator

In collaboration with the IRR of Lay-Saint-Christophe

As part of the second year project of my 2 years degree in computer science, in collaboration with the Regional Institute of Rehabilitation of Lay-Saint-Christophe, I had as a project manager to manage a team of 2 people, Nicolas Motel developer of the interface of the web application and Louis Thirion developer/designer of the prototype of connected gloves. We had to work on the realization of a web application and a prototype allowing the follow-up of the evolution of the rehabilitation of the fingers of the patients of the IRR, thanks to the various exercises created by the doctors in the application. As a developer I developed the application settings GUI, as well as the backend part of the web application.
